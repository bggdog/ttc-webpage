About the series

"Everyone here is doing their best. That's the most alarming part."

Shot in the tradition of the great workplace mockumentaries, The Therapy Company follows a rotating cast of well-meaning professionals whose personal dysfunction is only slightly more pronounced than the organizational chaos around them. The camera never blinks. Neither do they.
